Total Coins

The total supply of Meme Coin (MEME) is 69,911,000 tokens. This amount is fixed and will never be increased. Meme Coin is designed as a community-driven token, with transparency and fairness at its core.

Out of the total supply, the creator will retain 5,000,000 MEME tokens. These tokens are reserved for:

  • supporting the project in the long term,
  • marketing and collaborations,
  • and ensuring stability during the development phase.

The remaining 64,911,000 MEME tokens will be made available to the community through:

  • Liquidity Pools (such as Uniswap)
  • Community rewards
  • Airdrops
  • Future partnerships or use cases

This distribution ensures a fair launch and allows space for healthy growth of the project, fully in the hands of the community.

How To Buy Meme Coins

  • Open MetaMask and switch to the Ethereum Mainnet.
  • Make sure you have enough ETH in your wallet.
  • Go to Uniswap.org.
  • Click “Select Token” and paste the official MEME Coin contract address (to be announced at launch).
  • Enter how much ETH you want to trade for MEME Coin.
  • Click Swap and approve the transaction in MetaMask.
  • Wait a few seconds — your MEME Coin will appear in your wallet!
  • Always verify the contract address and leave some ETH for gas fees.
